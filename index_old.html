At Wittyhacks, we strive to bring together developers to participate in the hackathon irrespective of gender, race, sexual orientation, socioeconomic background or ethnicity. We usually try to keep things simple. Read our FAQs to phase out ambiguity.

  • Who can participate?

    Everyone is welcome to apply, be it students, professionals or turing-test certified androids. If you are under 18 years of age, we’ll need a parental consent form.

  • How does the application process work?

    We’re looking for people who "do"! Folks who are passionate enough to work on crazy world-changing ideas. Tell us what makes you apply to Wittyhacks, what excites you, accomplishments you are proud of and whatever else you think can strengthen your chances of acceptance. We’ll get to know more about your abilities from the past projects, GitHub profile, participation/awards in other hackathons.

  • Can we apply as a team?

    Yes! This hackathon is strictly a team competition. We believe that you’re stronger as a team than you are apart. You can join or create a team once you've started an individual application.

  • What if I don’t have a team or an idea?

    Not to worry, most people don’t! We’ll have team formation and ideation events geared towards helping you find people to work with. You can join our Facebook Group to find potential teammates.

  • What shouldn’t I bring?

    Weapons of any kind, drugs, or alcohol. If you’re not sure whether something will be okay, please ask ahead of time!

  • What about hardware?

    If you have specific hardware in mind, bring it with you. We will be doing our best to lend hardware at the event and enable you to work on your dream hack. We’ll put out a list closer to the event so you can see what’s available.

  • I don’t want to hack, can I still get involved?

    If you're looking to be a volunteer or mentor (i.e. someone with technical skills who'd be willing to help out participants during the weekend), you should reach out to us at Volunteer Registration Form. If your company is interested in becoming a sponsor, Fill up the Sponsorship form.

  • When will applications close?

    The application portal will be closed on January 10th at 11:59pm IST. We evaluate applications on a rolling basis and acceptances will be sent every weekend via email. The sooner you apply, the better chances you have of acceptance. Once accepted, you’ll have one day to confirm your attendance.

  • How much does it cost?

    Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. Admission to Wittyhacks is completely free, thanks to our sponsors! We'll provide you with a weekend's worth of schwag, meals, drinks, snacks and a place to crash when you need a break from coding.

  • How big can a team be?

    You can form teams of up to 4 people. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and developer skills.

  • Once I am accepted, what do I need to bring?

    A valid government issued photo ID, laptop, phone, chargers, any sleeping equipment you might want and a geeky t-shirt!

  • Do you provide travel reimbursements?

    No .!

  • What do I get by attending Wittyhacks?

    Pretty much everything from socializing with fellow hacker community to internship/job offers from the coolest tech companies. And yeah we’ll have prizes for 1st, 2nd, 3rd place, as well as the best hardware hack, and many other company sponsored prizes. Most of all, you will gain an unparalleled learning experience and memories for a lifetime!
